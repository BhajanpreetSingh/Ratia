Ratia

According to Census 2011 information the location code or village code of Ratia village is 125051. Ratia village is located in Fatehabad Tehsil of Fatehabad district in Haryana, India.The Ratia Municipal Committee has population of 37,852 of which 19,937 are males while 17,915 are females. Population of Children with age of 0-6 is 4683 which is 12.60% of total population of Ratia (MC). Female Sex Ratio is of 911 against state average of 879. Moreover, Child Sex Ratio in Ratia is around 846 compared to Haryana state average of 834.Literacy rate of Ratia city is 72.89% lower than state average of 75.55%.Male literacy is around 78.28% while female literacy rate is 67.04%. Jindal family originated from Budhlada lives here since 19th century.
