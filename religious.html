Door Of Salvation Church Ratia.

The doors of all Catholic churches, while artistic additions to the architecture, carry a far deeper symbolic meaning. The term the sheep gate that we hear of in the Scriptures was a special gate in Jerusalem through which the sacrificial sheep were brought to the temple. The sheep gate had no locks or bars, for all those of devout sincerity were welcome to bring sacrifice. This gate was blessed, setting it apart as a special gate. During this season of Easter, we recall in a particular way that Jesus passed through the sheep gate as the Lamb of God.

Baba Peer Dargah
